Why creatures are their own exporter (not the feats table)

Backgrounds and races are feat-family Items — they reuse the feat plumbing. Creatures are Actors (type:"npc") with a full stat block and their own embedded items (actions/attacks/spells). That's the concrete evidence for why they can't live in the feats table and need a dedicated creatures table — the export makes that visible. Scoped to npc only; PCs / vehicles / groups stay on the existing generic Export Actor Folder.

The honest caveat

Not yet runtime-tested in a live Foundry world. Both files syntax-check clean and the creature exporter mirrors the proven feat/actor exporters, but I can't drive Foundry headlessly — it needs real NPC actors present to verify end-to-end. With the junction now pointed at this branch, reload Foundry, drop a few monsters in a folder, and hit Export Creature Folder; if anything's off in the JSON, tell me and I'll fix it.
